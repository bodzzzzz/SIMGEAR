Frequently Asked Questions

Orders & Shipping

How long will it take to receive my order?
Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days after your order is processed. Expedited shipping takes 2-3 business days. Large fitness equipment may require additional time for delivery. You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship to select international destinations. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. Please note that customers are responsible for any customs duties or import taxes that may apply.
Can I modify or cancel my order after it's placed?
We process orders quickly to ensure fast shipping. If you need to modify or cancel your order, please contact our customer service team immediately. We'll do our best to accommodate your request, but we cannot guarantee changes once an order has entered the fulfillment process.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and in resalable condition. Some products, including personal fitness accessories and nutrition products, may not be eligible for return due to health and safety regulations. Please see our Shipping & Returns page for complete details.
How do I initiate a return?
To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team to receive a return authorization number. Without this number, we cannot process your return. Once approved, you'll receive return shipping instructions.
Do you offer exchanges?
Yes, we offer exchanges within the same 30-day window as returns. Contact our customer service team to arrange an exchange. For faster service, you may place a new order for the desired item and return the original purchase for a refund.

Products & Warranty

Do your products come with a warranty?
Yes, most of our fitness equipment comes with a manufacturer's warranty. Warranty periods vary by product category: machines typically have a 1-2 year warranty, while smaller accessories may have shorter coverage periods. Please check individual product pages for specific warranty information.
How do I assemble large fitness equipment?
All our fitness equipment comes with detailed assembly instructions. For larger items like treadmills and weight racks, we recommend having two people for assembly. If you prefer professional assembly, we can arrange this service for an additional fee in select areas.
Are your products suitable for home use?
Yes, our products are designed for both home and commercial use. We offer a range of equipment suitable for different space constraints and fitness levels. Product descriptions indicate whether items are best for beginners, intermediate, or advanced users.

Account & Orders

How do I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also log into your account on our website to view order status and tracking information.
Can I place an order without creating an account?
Yes, we offer guest checkout for your convenience. However, creating an account allows you to track orders, save favorite products, and enjoy a faster checkout process for future purchases.

Still have questions?

Our customer service team is here to help. Contact us at support@simgear.com or call (555) 123-4567 during business hours (Monday-Friday, 9am-5pm EST).
