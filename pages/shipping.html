Shipping & Returns

Shipping Policy

At SIMGEAR, we strive to provide fast, reliable shipping for all your fitness equipment needs. Here's what you need to know about our shipping process:

  • Processing Time: Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days.
  • Shipping Methods: We offer standard shipping (5-7 business days) and expedited shipping (2-3 business days).
  • Free Shipping: Orders over $100 qualify for free standard shipping within the continental United States.
  • International Shipping: We ship to select international destinations. Additional fees and customs duties may apply.
  • Tracking: A tracking number will be provided via email once your order ships.

Note: Large fitness equipment (treadmills, weight racks, etc.) may require special shipping arrangements and longer delivery times. You will be contacted with details after placing your order.

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not, we offer a straightforward return process:

  • Return Window: Items may be returned within 30 days of delivery.
  • Condition: Products must be unused, in original packaging, and in resalable condition.
  • Return Process: Contact our customer service team to initiate a return and receive a return authorization number.
  • Refunds: Once we receive and inspect your return, a refund will be issued to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.
  • Return Shipping: Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or was shipped in error.

Exceptions: Certain items, including personal fitness accessories, nutrition products, and clearance items, may not be eligible for return due to health and safety regulations.

Exchanges

Need a different size or color? We're happy to help with exchanges:

  • Exchanges follow the same 30-day policy as returns.
  • Contact customer service to arrange an exchange.
  • For faster service, you may place a new order for the desired item and return the original purchase for a refund.
